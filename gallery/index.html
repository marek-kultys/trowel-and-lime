Case study 1

The Old Forest

  • LOCATIONShoreditch, London

  • YEAR2017

  • SIZE8.3 m2 (50 sq ft)

  • TURNAROUND6 weeks

A young family living in East London asked Trowel & Lime to decorate a brick wall in their newly landscaped garden. The sgraffito is visible from their bedroom window, allowing them to start a day with an view on a fantastic forest.

Case study 4

Bathroom Pond

  • LOCATIONWarsaw, Poland

  • YEAR2007

  • SIZE1.5 m2 (16 sq ft)

  • TURNAROUNDUnder 1 weeks

Sgraffitos can be small gems too. A young family living in a modernist flat in central Warsaw wanted to do something unique to decorate their bathroom.

Trowel & Lime designed a multi-colour sgraffito depicting reed bed plants to take the bathing experience closer to the nature.
