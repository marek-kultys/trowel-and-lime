Case study 1

The Old Forest

  • LOCATION Shoreditch, London

  • YEAR 2017

  • SIZE 8.3 square metres (50 square feet)

  • TURNAROUND 6 weeks

A young family living in East London asked Trowel & Lime to decorate a brick wall in their newly landscaped garden. The sgraffito is visible from their bedroom window, allowing them to start a day with a view on a fantastic forest.

Case study 3

The White Forest

  • LOCATION Warsaw, Poland

  • YEAR 2007

  • SIZE 6.7 m2 (72 sq ft)

  • TURNAROUND About 5 weeks

When a family of four moved from a city flat to a new suburbian home, they asked Trowel & Lime to bring character to their new empty walls.

Organic shapes of sgraffito trees and a range of grainy textures give natural feel to a geometric, uniformely-finished interior.

Case study 4

Bathroom Pond

  • LOCATION Warsaw, Poland

  • YEAR 2007

  • SIZE 1.5 m2 (16 sq ft)

  • TURNAROUND Under 1 weeks

Sgraffitos can be small gems too. A young family living in a flat in central Warsaw wanted to do something unique to decorate their bathroom.

Trowel & Lime designed a multi-colour sgraffito depicting reed bed plants to take the bathing experience closer to nature.
