Case study 1

The Old Forest

  • LOCATIONShoreditch, London

  • YEAR2017

  • SIZE8.3 m2 (50 sq ft)

  • TURNAROUND6 weeks

A young family living in East London asked Trowel & Lime to decorate a brick wall in their newly landscaped garden. The sgraffito is visible from their bedroom window, allowing them to start a day with an view on a fantastic forest.

Case study 3

The White Forest

  • LOCATIONWarsaw, Poland

  • YEAR2007

  • SIZE6.7 m2 (72 sq ft)

  • TURNAROUNDabout 5 weeks

When a family of four moved from a city flat to a new suburbian home, they asked Trowel & Lime to help them bring some character to the untouched walls. Elaborate shapes of sgraffito trees and a spectrum of grainy textures of lime render brought unique natural quality to an uniformely-finished, geometric interior.
